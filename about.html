About Prime Touch Spa

Dedicated to providing the highest quality massage therapy and spa services in Islamabad since our founding.

Our Story

Prime Touch Spa was founded with a simple mission: to bring world-class massage therapy and spa services to Islamabad. We recognized that in today's fast-paced world, true relaxation and self-care are not luxuries—they are necessities for a balanced, healthy life.

Located in the heart of F-7 Markaz, our spa offers a serene escape from the bustle of city life. Every element of Prime Touch Spa has been thoughtfully designed to create an atmosphere of tranquility and well-being, from the calming interiors to the carefully curated selection of natural oils and therapies.

Our team consists of internationally certified therapists who bring a wealth of knowledge and experience from around the world. We combine traditional massage techniques with modern wellness practices to deliver treatments that are both therapeutic and deeply relaxing.
